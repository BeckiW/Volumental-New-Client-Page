New Balance

New Balance is known not just for the quality of their footwear, but for their vast range of widths and sizes. In summer 2016, Volumental and New Balance teamed up to provide customers with the ultimate in-store experience, integrating Volumental’s in-store scanning solution with New Balance's proprietary gait analysis technology, “StrideID”. Together, New Balance and Volumental ensure a faster and smoother fitting process in-store.

To quote Jonathan Clarke, International Retail Director: "Our customers love it and our staff love it."

“New Balance is at the forefront of 3D printing and has been utilizing this exciting and innovative technology to customize product for our athletes for a number of years,” says New Balance President and CEO Robert DeMartini. “Our unique position as both a manufacturer and retailer allows us to bring the world’s first 3D printed running midsole to market. The Zante Generate demonstrates the strength of New Balance design and innovation and will allow consumers to own a piece of running technology history.”
